# E-Commerce Implementation Service

## What it is
- End-to-end ecommerce store implementation.
- Product catalog, payments, and shipping setup.
- Admin training and analytics configuration.

## Who it is for
- Brands launching their first store.
- Teams migrating to a new platform.
- Founders who need hands-on setup help.

## What's included
- Store design and configuration.
- Payment gateway integration.
- Shipping and tax setup guidance.
- Order management and email flows.
- Admin training materials.

## What it is NOT
- Ongoing marketing or ad management.
- Third-party fulfillment services.
- Unlimited revisions without scope.
