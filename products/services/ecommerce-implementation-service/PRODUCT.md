# E-Commerce Implementation Service Spec

## Features
- Custom store design.
- Payments and shipping configuration.
- Customer accounts and notifications.
- Analytics setup and reporting.
- Post-launch support.

## Constraints
- Payment provider must be agreed upfront.
- Timeline depends on product catalog size.
- Requires client access to assets and data.

## Assumptions
- Client provides product data and images.
- Shipping regions are defined early.
- Compliance requirements are shared.

## Open questions
- Which payment provider is preferred?
- Are there subscription products?
- What are the target shipping regions?
