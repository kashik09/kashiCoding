# Technical Consulting

## What it is
- Consulting engagement for code and architecture.
- Performance, security, and DevOps guidance.
- Team training and documentation reviews.

## Who it is for
- Teams needing a second opinion on architecture.
- Founders who want a focused audit.
- Engineering teams seeking training.

## What's included
- Audit and review reports.
- Recommendations and action plans.
- Workshops or training sessions.
- Follow-up review sessions.
- Templates for tracking improvements.

## What it is NOT
- Ongoing engineering staffing.
- Unlimited refactors included.
- Legal or compliance certification.
