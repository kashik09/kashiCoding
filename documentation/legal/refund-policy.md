# Refund Policy

Short version: no refunds after purchase or payment, except where required by law.

## Digital Products
- All digital products are one-time purchases and delivered as downloads.
- No refunds after purchase, delivery, or license issuance.

## Memberships, Subscriptions, and Credits
- Memberships and subscriptions are non-refundable.
- Credits are prepaid service units, not currency, not hours, and have no cash value.
- Credits are usable while valid and expire based on plan terms. Unused credits are forfeited at expiration.

## Services
- Service payments (credit-based or cash) are non-refundable once work is scheduled or started.
- Scope changes require additional credits or payment.

## Legal Exceptions
If local law requires a refund, we will comply. Otherwise, all sales are final.

## Chargebacks
Chargebacks or payment reversals may result in immediate suspension of access and cancellation of licenses or credits while the dispute is investigated.
