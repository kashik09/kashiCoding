# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

This policy applies to the website, digital products, and services. It exists to protect customers and keep the platform reliable.

## Allowed Use
- Use products and services for lawful, internal purposes within the license you purchased.
- Share access only within your licensed team limit.
- Use service credits for scoped work that we both agreed to.

## Prohibited Use
- Sharing, reselling, sublicensing, or redistributing any digital product.
- Bypassing license checks, download limits, or security controls.
- Account sharing, credential stuffing, scraping, or automated abuse.
- Uploading malicious files, running attacks, or probing for vulnerabilities.
- Misrepresenting identity, age, or eligibility (including student status).
- Using the platform to build competing license bypass tools or piracy systems.

## Enforcement
- We log downloads, license actions, and abuse signals for security and audit purposes.
- Abuse or violations may result in license suspension, account freeze, or termination.
- Chargebacks or payment disputes can lead to immediate access suspension.

## Reporting Issues
If you see abuse or suspect a security issue, contact us via the site contact form.
