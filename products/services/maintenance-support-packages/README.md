# Maintenance & Support Packages

## What it is
- Monthly maintenance and support packages.
- Bug fixes, updates, and monitoring.
- Priority support tiers.

## Who it is for
- Teams without internal maintenance capacity.
- Clients who need ongoing updates.
- Projects that require reliability.

## What's included
- Bug fixes and minor updates.
- Security and dependency updates.
- Performance monitoring and reports.
- Priority support based on tier.
- Monthly status summary.

## What it is NOT
- Unlimited feature development.
- Major redesigns or rebuilds.
- 24/7 emergency support unless specified.
