## SECTION 1: STUDENT & YOUTH DISCOUNT PROGRAM

### 1.1 Eligibility

**Student Discount (Ages 18+):**
- Must be currently enrolled in an accredited educational institution
- Valid student email (.edu or institutional domain) OR student ID required
- Verification expires after 12 months
- Must reapply after expiration

**Youth Discount (Ages 13-18):**
- Must be between 13-18 years of age
- Requires guardian email and explicit guardian consent
- Guardian must approve the application
- Verification expires after 12 months
- Stricter scope boundaries apply

### 1.2 Verification Process

1. **Application Submission:**
   - User submits verification request via /student page
   - Provides institution name, country, and proof of enrollment
   - Proof methods: school email, student ID upload, or manual documentation

2. **Manual Review:**
   - All applications are manually reviewed by administrators
   - Review time: 1-2 business days
   - Approval/rejection notification sent via email

3. **Approval:**
   - Discount active for 12 months from approval date
   - 50% discount applied automatically at checkout
   - User notified with expiration date

4. **Rejection:**
   - User notified with rejection reason
   - 24-hour cooldown before reapplication
   - May resubmit with additional documentation

### 1.3 Discount Terms

- **Discount Amount:** 50% off personal-tier products and services
- **Valid For:** Personal use only (no team or commercial licenses)
- **Duration:** 12 months from approval
- **Renewal:** Must reapply after expiration
- **Restrictions:**
  - Stricter scope boundaries than regular personal tier
  - Additional revisions cost extra
  - No refunds on discounted purchases
  - Discount cannot be combined with other promotions

### 1.4 Fraud & Misrepresentation

**Zero-Tolerance Policy:**

If fraudulent information is detected:
1. **Immediate account suspension**
2. **Permanent ban from student program**
3. **Revocation of all licenses**
4. **No refunds issued**

Examples of fraud:
- Fake student IDs or enrollment letters
- Using someone else's student credentials
- Misrepresenting age (for youth discount)
- Falsifying guardian consent

**We reserve the right to:**
- Request additional verification at any time
- Suspend accounts pending investigation
- Report fraudulent activities to authorities
- Pursue legal action for material fraud

## SECTION 2: ANTI-RESALE & LICENSE ENFORCEMENT

### 2.1 License Restrictions

All digital product licenses include these restrictions:

**PERSONAL LICENSE:**
- Single user only
- Personal or limited commercial use (your own projects)
- **NO RESALE** — Cannot sell, sublicense, or distribute the product
- **NO SHARING** — Cannot share download links or files with others
- **NO REDISTRIBUTION** — Cannot include in bundles, marketplaces, or public repositories
- Maximum 1 active device/installation

**TEAM LICENSE:**
- 2-5 users maximum (hard cap, explicitly assigned)
- Internal team use only within one organization
- **NO RESALE** — Cannot sell or sublicense to external parties
- **NO REDISTRIBUTION** — Cannot share outside the licensed team
- Seats must be explicitly assigned and tracked

**COMMERCIAL LICENSE:**
- Extended commercial rights (specific use cases defined per product)
- **NO RESALE** — Cannot resell the product itself as a standalone item
- **NO REDISTRIBUTION** — Cannot redistribute source files
- May use in client projects (with restrictions specified per product)

### 2.2 Prohibited Activities

The following are STRICTLY PROHIBITED and will result in immediate suspension:

1. **Reselling Products:**
   - Selling licenses or products to others
   - Listing products on marketplaces (Gumroad, Envato, etc.)
   - Including products in paid bundles or packages

2. **Sharing Downloads:**
   - Posting download links publicly
   - Sharing files via file-sharing services
   - Distributing copies to non-licensed users

3. **License Key Sharing:**
   - Posting license keys publicly (forums, social media, etc.)
   - Sharing license keys with non-authorized users
   - Selling or trading license keys

4. **Fraudulent Usage:**
   - Creating multiple accounts to bypass restrictions
   - Using stolen payment information
   - Abusing download limits
   - Circumventing technical restrictions

### 2.3 Abuse Detection & Enforcement

**Automated Monitoring:**

We employ automated systems to detect suspicious behavior, including:
- Excessive downloads from multiple IPs/devices
- Download patterns consistent with resale/redistribution
- Multiple accounts using similar payment identifiers
- Public sharing of license keys or download links

**Suspicion Scoring:**

Each license has a "suspicion score" based on:
- Download frequency (>10 downloads in 24 hours = flagged)
- Unique IP addresses (>5 IPs in 24 hours = flagged)
- Device count for personal licenses (>3 devices = flagged)
- Failed download patterns (possible scraping)

**Thresholds:**
- **Score 50-69:** Manual admin review required
- **Score 70-100:** Automatic account suspension + license revocation

### 2.4 Account Suspension Process

**Automatic Suspension:**

If suspicion score >= 70:
1. Account immediately suspended
2. All active licenses revoked
3. User notified via email with reason
4. Audit log entry created for review

**Manual Suspension:**

Admin may suspend accounts for:
- Confirmed resale activity
- Public license key sharing
- Terms of Service violations
- Fraudulent verification applications

**Effects of Suspension:**
- Cannot download any products
- Cannot make new purchases
- Cannot access member areas
- No refunds for suspended accounts

**Appeal Process:**
- Email support with explanation and evidence
- Admin reviews case within 3-5 business days
- If legitimate error: account reactivated
- If confirmed violation: permanent ban

### 2.5 License Revocation

**Grounds for Revocation:**
1. Account suspension (automatic)
2. Resale detected
3. Violation of license terms
4. Fraudulent acquisition
5. Chargeback/payment dispute

**Effects of Revocation:**
- Download access removed immediately
- License marked as REVOKED in system
- No future downloads permitted
- Previously downloaded files should be destroyed (honor system)

**No Refunds:**
- Revoked licenses are not refundable
- Violations forfeit all payment rights
- No partial refunds for unused time

## SECTION 3: DATA HANDLING FOR VERIFICATION

### 3.1 Information Collected

For student/youth verification, we collect:
- Institution name and country
- School email address (if email verification method)
- Student ID or enrollment documentation (if upload method)
- Guardian email and consent (for youth 13-18)
- Verification status and review notes

### 3.2 Data Retention

- **Active Verifications:** Retained indefinitely while status is APPROVED
- **Expired Verifications:** Retained for 24 months after expiration
- **Rejected Verifications:** Retained for 12 months (cooldown enforcement)
- **Uploaded Documents:** Retained for verification period + 12 months

### 3.3 Data Access

- **Admin Access:** Limited to ADMIN and OWNER roles
- **User Access:** Users can view their own verification status
- **Third Parties:** Never shared with third parties
- **Deletion Requests:** Can request deletion after expiration (subject to audit requirements)

### 3.4 Security

- Uploaded documents stored securely (encrypted at rest)
- Access logged in audit trail
- No public exposure of verification data
- Guardian information kept confidential

## SECTION 4: REFUND POLICY UPDATES

### 4.1 Digital Products

- **Standard Policy:** No refunds after download (digital goods exemption)
- **Exception:** Technical issues preventing download (case-by-case)
- **Student Discounts:** No refunds on discounted purchases
- **Revoked Licenses:** No refunds for violations

### 4.2 Memberships & Credits

- **Cancellation:** Can cancel anytime, no future charges
- **Prepaid Credits:** Non-refundable (except in cases of fraud by seller)
- **Unused Credits:** No cash value, expire with membership term
- **Suspended Accounts:** No refunds for suspension due to violations

### 4.3 Services

- **Fixed-Price Projects:** Refunds on case-by-case basis before work starts
- **Subscriptions:** Pro-rated refund for unused months (discretionary)
- **Completed Work:** No refunds after final delivery accepted
