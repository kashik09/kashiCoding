# Custom Portfolio Development Spec

## Features
- Custom design and build.
- CMS integration as needed.
- Contact forms and SEO tuning.
- Performance optimization targets.
- Post-launch support window.

## Constraints
- Scope defined in statement of work.
- Timeline depends on content readiness.
- Support limited to agreed window.

## Assumptions
- Client provides assets and content.
- Feedback cycles are scheduled.
- Hosting choice is agreed early.

## Open questions
- Which CMS does the client prefer?
- Who owns ongoing content updates?
- Is multilingual support required?
