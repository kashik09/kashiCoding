# E-Commerce Starter Kit

## What it is
- Complete ecommerce starter with catalog, cart, and checkout.
- Stripe-based payment integration and order flow.
- Admin tools for products and orders.

## Who it is for
- Founders launching a digital product store.
- Agencies needing a reusable ecommerce base.
- Developers who want a ready-to-extend stack.

## What's included
- Product catalog with filtering and search.
- Shopping cart with session persistence.
- Stripe payments and webhook hooks.
- Admin dashboard for products and orders.
- Multi-currency configuration baseline.

## What it is NOT
- Merchant onboarding or payment account setup.
- A hosted checkout service.
- Fulfillment operations or logistics.
